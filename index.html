Pais

Your Intelligent Calendar Assistant

Turn Anything Into Calendar Events

Pais uses advanced AI to extract calendar events from any format—photos of posters, voice commands, text messages, or documents. No more manual data entry.

  • Upload a photo of a concert flyer—Pais creates the calendar event
  • Say "Dentist appointment next Tuesday at 2pm"—it's scheduled
  • Paste meeting notes with dates—all events extracted automatically
  • Ask Pais to find event schedules online—it searches and imports them

Universal input. Intelligent extraction. Your calendar, simplified.
